About Me

Academic Journey & Expertise

A dedicated biochemist committed to advancing scientific knowledge and education

Ahmed Gamal Khamis Abdelnabi is an Assistant Lecturer at the Chemistry Department, Faculty of Science, Sohag University, Egypt.

He holds an M.Sc. in Biochemistry and is currently pursuing his PhD degree. His B.Sc. in Chemistry was completed with Excellent grade and Highest Distinction of Honours (87.54%), ranking first over the whole class.

His research interests span biochemistry, toxicology, natural products, drug design, and molecular docking with a focus on understanding and mitigating the toxic effects of environmental contaminants.
